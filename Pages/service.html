Services

A range of architectural services, guiding projects from initial concept through to completion. Our approach combines thoughtful design, technical expertise, and clear communication to deliver well-resolved, practical, and sustainable solutions tailored to each client and site.

Dimensional survey

Carry out accurate dimensional surveys to inform the design process, providing reliable measured drawings as a foundation for planning and technical development.

Master planning

Develop strategic master planning proposals that consider movement, land use, and long-term growth to create cohesive, adaptable, and people-focused environments.

Retrofit

Provide retrofit solutions focused on improving energy performance, comfort, and longevity, upgrading existing buildings while reducing environmental impact.

Extensions

Design thoughtful extensions that enhance existing homes, improving space, light, and functionality while responding sensitively to the character of the original building.

New build

Deliver bespoke new-build projects, guiding clients from concept design through to technical detailing to create well-considered, buildable, and high-quality architecture.

Planning permission

Manage the planning process from initial feasibility through to submission, preparing clear design proposals and supporting documentation to help secure planning approval.
