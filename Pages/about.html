About us

ALNBM is a design-led architectural practice passionate about creating spaces that work beautifully for the people who use them. From residential extensions to larger commercial and industrial projects, we bring creativity, clarity, and care to every stage of the design process.

Our Focus

Our focus is on delivering thoughtful, well-resolved architecture that balances design quality, functionality, and sustainability. We prioritise clear communication, careful detailing, and a deep understanding of each client's needs to create spaces that are efficient, enduring, and responsive to their context.
